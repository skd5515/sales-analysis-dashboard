# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover actionable business insights related to revenue trends, profitability, customer behavior, and discount strategies. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying patterns, inefficiencies, and growth opportunities within the sales pipeline.


## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze historical sales data to identify key performance drivers
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on revenue and profit
* Understand regional and category-wise sales performance
* Build an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders


## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **Microsoft Excel**

  * Pivot Tables
  * Data Cleaning & Transformation
  * Advanced Formulas (IF, XLOOKUP, etc.)
* **Data Visualization**

  * Charts & KPI Cards
* **Analytical Thinking**

  * Business problem solving
  * Insight generation


## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales records with the following key attributes:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Customer Name
* Region
* Product Category & Sub-Category
* Sales Amount
* Discount (%)
* Profit


## ❓ Key Business Questions

* Which region generates the highest revenue and profit?
* How do discounts impact profitability?
* Which product categories drive maximum sales?
* What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?
* Who are the top contributing customers?


## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Removed missing and inconsistent values
* Standardized date and numerical formats
* Verified data integrity and removed duplicates
* Created calculated metrics:

  * Profit Margin
  * Discount Buckets
  * Revenue Aggregations

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance:

### 🔹 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Discount

### 🔹 Visual Insights

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Profit vs Discount Analysis

### 🔹 Filters & Interactivity

* Region Filter
* Category Filter
* Time Period Selection

## 🔍 Key Insights

* The **West region** contributes the highest share of overall revenue
* High discount levels (>25%) significantly reduce profit margins
* The **Technology category** generates the most revenue but requires margin optimization
* Certain customer segments contribute disproportionately to total sales

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies to avoid profit leakage
* Focus on high-performing regions and replicate strategies across weaker regions
* Improve pricing strategies for low-margin categories
* Target high-value customers with personalized offers
